How to review performance

  1. Use at least 3 independent raters.
  2. Score hook recall, emotional immediacy, clip usability, replay tension, and lane fit on a 1–5 scale.
  3. Weighted total formula: 6×hook + 5×emotion + 4×clip + 3×replay + 2×lane.
  4. Promote only demos at 75+ with hook/emotion/clip means all at 4+ and no originality concern.
