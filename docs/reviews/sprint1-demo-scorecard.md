## Rubric

Scored 1-5 on each axis.

- **Memorability** — is there a quotable or instantly sticky hook phrase?
- **Title strength** — does the title feel usable, searchable, and emotionally clear?
- **Emotional clarity** — is the feeling obvious on first exposure?
- **First-10-sec clip pull** — does the writing pack imply a strong short-form opening?
- **Lane fit** — does the demo clearly belong to a marketable lane?
- **Replay intent** — would this concept earn another listen based on the hook alone?

## Rater definitions

- **Rater A — Commercial hook pass:** prioritizes memorability and mainstream lift.
- **Rater B — Lyric/identity pass:** prioritizes quotable language and emotional point of view.
- **Rater C — Clip-first pass:** prioritizes short-form opening and visual reuse potential.
